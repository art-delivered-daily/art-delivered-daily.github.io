### Standing Bather, Seen from the Back
 
**Artist**<br>
Paul Cezanne (French, 1839–1906)
 
**Date**<br>
1879-82
 
**Medium**<br>
Oil on canvas
 
**Dimensions**<br>
31.7 × 21.6 cm (12 1/2 × 8 1/2 in.); Framed: 53.4 × 42.3 × 8.9 cm (21 × 16 5/8 × 3 1/2 in.)
 
**Category**<br>
Painting
 
**Style**<br>
19th century
 
**Description**<br>
When making an artwork, Cezanne often significantly altered his original design. This canvas was cut from a larger painting, retaining remnants of the other composition. In the lower-left corner, a series of blue lines might be legs of a seated figure; the body may have continued on another part of the canvas. These lines on top of the background foliage, indicate that he added a second figure after completing the first, before changing his mind.
