### The Diversion of the Euphrates from The Story of Cyrus
 
**Artist**<br>
Adapted from designs by Michiel Coxie (1499–1592)<br>
Woven at the workshop of Gillis Ydens<br>
Brussels
 
**Date**<br>
c. 1670
 
**Medium**<br>
Wool and silk, slit and single interlocking tapestry weave
Warp: Count: 8 warps per cm; wool: S-ply of three Z-spun elements; diameters: 0.6–1.0 mm
Weft: Count: varies from 19 to 44 wefts per cm; wool: S-ply of two Z-spun elements; diameters: 0.3–1.5 mm; silk: pairs of S-ply of two Z-twisted elements; diameters: 0.15–0.5 mm
 
**Dimensions**<br>
325.4 × 404.8 cm (128 1/8 × 159 3/8 in.)
 
**Category**<br>
Textile
